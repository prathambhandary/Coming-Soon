A Life Remembered

We gather here today in solemn remembrance of our dear Bhowmik, who departed this mortal realm shockingly, suddenly, and without any warning whatsoever.

Those who knew him remember his unique laugh, his questionable fashion choices, and his remarkable ability to eat an ungodly amount of food and still claim to be hungry.

He is survived by his phone (which probably has 3% battery right now), his overdue assignments, and a half-eaten packet of chips that no one has the heart to throw away.

The world is a darker, quieter place without his unsolicited opinions.
